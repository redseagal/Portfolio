Visual Communications Projects



Hi, Dad! Electronic Press Kit (EPK)

This electronic press kit was prepared for the film, Hi, Dad, produced by Luwen Shao. The short film is about a boy who has heard all his life that his father is a fireman. Now seeking the truth of who his father is, he starts to set things on fire, hoping to reunite with his fireman dad.



Metro Coffee Shop

For the web page design project, I created a mock coffee shop website. I was looking to create something clean and simple, only focusing the coffee products. The type is either in black or white and the design is very minimal. The pictures I took all have a marble gray background making the coffee and matcha tea stand out.



Wedding Bells Rings

For this layout project, story and photos were provided for the purpose of creating a news article layout. I choose the photos that were most celebratory in reference to the story line. I wanted to center the photos to create a nice balance throughout the layout.



Latinos in Space

I chose this topic for my infographic because I had just recently learned about Latino astronauts by the way of looking up curious information. There are many Latinos that have been to space but for the sake of space, (no pun intended) I referenced only the first of their country or ethnic back, respectively.
